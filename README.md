## üéØ Problem Statement
Dead stock leads to:
- Financial losses due to expired medicines  
- Increased storage costs  
- Inefficient inventory utilization  
- Supply chain imbalance  

The goal of this project is to **predict the risk level of dead stock** using historical inventory and demand data, allowing companies to take **preventive actions** instead of reactive ones.

---

## Solution Approach
We developed a **risk prediction model** that:
- Analyzes historical sales and inventory patterns  
- Identifies products with low movement or high expiry risk  
- Predicts the probability of a product becoming dead stock  

This helps decision-makers optimize:
- Inventory replenishment  
- Production planning  
- Distribution strategies
